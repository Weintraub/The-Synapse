Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get all the answers to the most frequently asked questions (FAQs) regarding the conference. Click the question to see the answer!

  • Who can register for the conference?

    • The conference is open to all public and independent high school students in Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.

  • How do I register for the conference?

    • Click here to register!

  • How much does admission cost?

    • It's free!

  • Will tickets be sold at the door?

    • Unfortunately, all attendees must register on EventBrite beforehand as we cannot accept new registrations at the door.

  • Where do I go once I arrive?

    • Head to the Upper School building (the building with the clock tower) and enter through the main entrance. You will then sign-in at the registration desk located in the main foyer. If you are entering from the north entrance (77 Kilbarry Road), please follow the directional signs to get to the main entrance of the building.

  • Will parking be available on campus?

    • Limited parking will be available on campus, so using public transportation is strongly encouraged. If you intend to arrive by car, please find a parking space in the William P. Wilder Arena & Sports Complex.

  • Is there a dress code?

    • Nope, casual dress is perfectly fine.

How to get to the conference:

  • Route 1 (North, South, or East)

    • 1. Subway to St. Clair Station
      2. Follow the signs to get onto the streetcar platform
      3. Get onto the 512 towards Keele
      4. Get off at Avenue Road West
      5. Walk up Avenue Road towards UCC (look for the clock tower)
      6. Walk up the driveway towards the clock tower and enter the glass doors

  • Route 2 (West)

    • 1. Subway to St. Clair West Station
      2. Take the 512 streetcar towards St. Clair Station
      3. Get off at Avenue Road West
      4. Walk up Avenue Road towards UCC (look for the clock tower)
      5. Walk up the driveway towards the clock tower and enter the glass doors

  • Route 3 (Central)

    • 1. Subway to Bay station
      2. Walk towards Avenue Road on Bloor
      3. Take the 5 bus towards Eglinton Station
      4. Get off at Lonsdale Road South Side
      5. Walk up the driveway towards the clock tower and enter the glass doors

  • Will lunch be provided?

    • A complimentary lunch and snacks will be provided at the conference. All food provided are nut-free and a vegetarian option is also available.

  • How can I get involved next year?

    • Information about applying for a position on the Executive Committee will be released mid-May.

  • Where do I go to if I have more questions?

    • There will be conference staff on duty at the conference to answer any questions you may have on the day of the event. You can also contact us by emailing thesynapseconference@gmail.com.
